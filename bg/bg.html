üìã Feature Description

Our Black & White Colorization tool provides powerful AI-powered capabilities to bring your old black and white photos to life:

  • Multiple Colorization Modes: Support auto colorization (recommended), manual colorization, and style colorization
  • Intelligent Recognition: Automatically recognizes image content and applies suitable colors
  • Color Style Options: Choose from natural, vintage, vivid, warm, or cool color styles
  • Manual Adjustments: Fine-tune skin tone, sky tone, plant tone, and building tone
  • Advanced Features: Preserve contrast, enhance details, smooth transitions, color harmony, skin detection, and object recognition

üí° Usage Guide

  1. Click to choose B&W photos or drag & drop them to upload (up to 5 photos)
  2. Select colorization mode: Auto colorization (recommended), Manual colorization, or Style colorization
  3. Adjust colorization strength using the slider or preset buttons (light, standard, or deep)
  4. Choose color style: Natural, Vintage, Vivid, Warm, or Cool
  5. If using manual mode, adjust skin tone, sky tone, plant tone, and building tone as needed
  6. Enable advanced settings: preserve contrast, enhance details, smooth transitions, color harmony, skin detection, object recognition
  7. Select output format: Keep original format, JPEG, PNG, or WebP
  8. Adjust output quality (60% to 100%)
  9. Click "Preview" to see the colorization effect
  10. Click "Start colorizing" to begin processing
  11. Download individual colorized photos or download all as a ZIP file
