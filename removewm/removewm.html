üí° Usage Guide

  1. Click "Choose images" or drag & drop images to upload (up to 5 images)
  2. Select removal method: AI Smart Removal (recommended), Manual Selection, or Auto Watermark Detection
  3. For AI removal: Adjust processing intensity and select watermark type
  4. For manual selection: Choose selection tool and repair algorithm, then select watermark area on the image
  5. For auto detection: Adjust detection sensitivity and select detection range
  6. Enable advanced settings if needed (preserve quality, enhance details, smooth edges, color matching)
  7. Choose output format (keep original, JPEG, PNG, or WebP) and quality
  8. Click "Remove Watermark" to process all images
  9. Download individual images or download all as ZIP
