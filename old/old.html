Our Old Photo Restoration tool provides powerful AI-powered capabilities to restore your old photos:

  • Multiple Repair Types: Support smart restoration, color restoration, damage repair, and noise reduction
  • Intelligent Detection: Automatically detects photo issues and applies optimal repair algorithms
  • Color Enhancement: Adjust saturation, contrast, and brightness to restore original colors
  • Detail Enhancement: Sharpening and detail enhancement to bring back lost details
  • Advanced Options: Auto color balance, noise reduction, edge enhancement, texture restoration, scratch and stain removal

üí° Usage Guide

  1. Click to select old photos or drag & drop them to upload (up to 5 photos)
  2. Choose repair type: Smart Restoration (recommended), Color Restoration, Damage Repair, or Noise Reduction
  3. Adjust repair intensity using the slider or preset buttons (light, standard, or strong)
  4. Fine-tune color enhancement: saturation, contrast, and brightness
  5. Adjust detail enhancement: sharpening and detail enhancement levels
  6. Enable advanced options as needed: auto color balance, noise reduction, edge enhancement, texture restoration, scratch removal, stain removal
  7. Select output format: Keep Original Format, JPEG, PNG, or WebP
  8. Adjust output quality (60% to 100%)
  9. Click "Preview Results" to see the restoration effect
  10. Click "Start Restoration" to begin processing
  11. Download individual restored photos or download all as a ZIP file
