Our Watermark tool provides powerful watermarking capabilities to protect your images:

  • Text Watermark: Add customizable text watermarks with font, size, color, and opacity controls
  • Image Watermark: Upload logo or icon as watermark with size and opacity adjustments
  • Multiple Positions: Choose from 9 preset positions or set custom coordinates
  • Style Effects: Add shadow, border, and rotation effects for professional-looking watermarks
  • Batch Processing: Add watermarks to up to 10 images at once

üí° Usage Guide

  1. Click "Choose image" or drag & drop images to upload (up to 10 images)
  2. Select watermark type: Text Watermark or Image Watermark
  3. For text watermark: Enter text, adjust font, size, color, and opacity
  4. For image watermark: Upload watermark image, adjust size and opacity
  5. Choose watermark position from preset options or set custom coordinates
  6. Enable style effects if needed (shadow, border, rotation)
  7. Choose output format (keep original, JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  8. Click "Add Watermark" to process all images
  9. Download individual images or download all as ZIP
