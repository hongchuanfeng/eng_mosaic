üìã Feature Description

Our Image Cropper tool provides a powerful and intuitive way to crop your images:

  • Drag to Crop: Click and drag to select the area you want to crop
  • Live Preview: See the cropped result in real-time as you adjust the selection
  • Move Tool: Reposition the crop area by dragging it around
  • Reset: Reset the crop area to the original image size
  • High Quality: Download your cropped image in original quality

üí° Usage Guide

  1. Click "Choose Image" or drag & drop an image to upload
  2. Select the "Crop" tool (default) to start cropping
  3. Click and drag on the image to select the area you want to keep
  4. Adjust the crop area by dragging the handles at the corners and edges
  5. Use the "Move" tool to reposition the crop area if needed
  6. Check the preview to see how your cropped image will look
  7. Click "Download cropped image" to save your result
